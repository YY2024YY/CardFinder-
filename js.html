Welcome to the World of Here to Slay

Here to Slay is a card game that is easy to learn and fun to play. It is a game of strategy and luck. The goal of the game is to be the first player to collect three creatures of the same color. The game is played with a deck of cards that have different creatures on them. Each creature has a color and a special ability. Players take turns drawing cards from the deck and playing them on the table. They can also use their cards to attack other players and steal their creatures. The game is fast-paced and exciting, with lots of twists and turns. It is a great game for people of all ages, and can be played with two to six players. So gather your friends and family, and get ready to have a blast playing Here to Slay!
